Summit To Share

Summit To Share is a infromation based wesbite for a project started by a group of primary school friends to support and spread awareness for mental health. The project is in support of the charity Ask For Jake. The main goal of Summit To Share is to encourage people to get outside and talk to one another and try and end the stigmatism of keeping our feelings to ourselfs. Some key features of this application are the following:

  • Information about the project itself.
  • Information on the charity with a link to the charities website.
  • A sign up form for a newsletter to find out about up and comiong events/hikes.
  • Links to the porjects instagram account and to donate.
